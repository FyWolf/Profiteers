🎮 MODPACK COMPARISON TOOL

Compare two Arma 3 modpack exports to identify added and removed mods.
Perfect for tracking modpack changes and creating changelogs.

📋 How to Use

  • Step 1: Export your old modpack as HTML from Arma 3 Launcher
  • Step 2: Export your new/updated modpack as HTML
  • Step 3: Upload both files below
  • Step 4: Click "Compare Modpacks" to see the differences
